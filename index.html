Bifidobacterium — 63,876%
PROTECTIVE WHAT TO KNOW
  • Bifidobacterium is a genus of beneficial bateria that are among the first to inhabit
    the gastrointestinal tract. They are seen in higher amounts in exclusively breastfed
    infants than in infants who have had formula introduced
  • Due to their proven clinical efficacy in infants, bifidobacterium is being used as probiotics.
  • Bifidobacteria play an important role in the prevention and treatment of
    pathologies typical of newborns, such as necrotizing enterocolitis, infantile colics,
    acute diarrhea, and neonatal infections.
  • Supplementation of bifidobacterium spp, strains help in the treatment and prevention
    of other pathologies like allergies, celiac disease, obesity, and neurological and
    psychiatric diseases in newborns and infants.
  • They are known to improve atopic eczema in infants and help gain weight
    in extremely low birth weight infants.
